Wellize (Mental Health/Wellness App)

Details

This app is designed to let users track their emotional and mental well-being. This app is built in a response to the outbreak of COVID-19 and its impact on the human condition, specifically the mind. Wellize is useful because, unlike competitors, it is entirely free to use. The philosophy here is that individuals who seek emotional and mental support need full access and do not need a roadblock, such as a financial burden.
